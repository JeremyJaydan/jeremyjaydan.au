Driving force

I originally went down the path of entrepreneurship to get rich quick, and a decade later I'm still on this path, albeit slow and not in it for the money. I've found a love for the craft, constantly up-skilling, learning, and solving. Even with a passion for the craft though I've still persistently asked myself why I'm doing this, what's the point?

I've written notes as I've come across different ideas of what it could be but it's my most recent note on the 27th of November that has struck a chord: agency is what matters. The ability to make my own decisions. I've thought about not working for someone as a reason to switch tracks but it seems it may also be a continuous driving force.

To be motivated by the ability to make one's own decisions is extremely liberating. I thought I needed to figure out point A to point B of product design and development but it seems all it took was just realizing that all I really want out of life is to make it my own.
